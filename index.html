Privacy Policy for SecurecheckPro

Last Updated: 13-12-2023

Introduction

Welcome to SecurecheckPro, an app dedicated to simplifying and securing the process of managing checks.

Data Collection and Use

Registration Information: For registration purposes, we collect your first name, last name, and RIB (Relevé d'Identité Bancaire). This information is used to create and manage your user account and to provide the necessary features of our app.

Check Management: Our platform allows users to manage their checks, including verifying the authenticity and generating unique tokens.

Purpose of Data Collection: The collected data is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving our services. It is not used for any other purposes or shared with third parties.

Data Sharing and Security

No Data Sharing: We do not share any data with third parties

Security Measures:

All data exchanged within our application is encrypted, ensuring the security of your sensitive information.

Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
